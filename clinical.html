Clinical Research Projects

Clinical Applications of Bioimpedance: Leveraging the knowledge gained from understanding contrast levels and mechanisms and the instrumentation designed and constructed to integrate these technologies into the clinical environment. Projects include:

  • Intraoperative Surgical Margin Assessment with Electrical Impedance Spectroscopy
  • Electrical Impedance-based Imaging of Brain Compliance in an Animal Model
  • Electrical Property-based Image-Guided Prostate Biopsy
  • Incorporating Electrical Property Sensing Into a Clinical Biopsy Needle for Enhanced Prostate Cancer Detection
